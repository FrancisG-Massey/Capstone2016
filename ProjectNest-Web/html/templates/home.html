What We're Doing

Project Nest is the development of a mobile application to aid volunteers of DOC with the resetting of traplines.

Four students from Massey University are undertaking the design, implementation, testing, and deployment of this application to satisfy the requirements of a Software Engineering capstone project.

We are working closely with volunteers and academic staff to provide a professional and successful cross-platform experience.
